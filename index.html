Hi,
I am thinking about conducting a 6 week pilot program making use of Khan Academy
During these 6 weeks, participants will get together for 3 times per week and assist each other. We will not have an instructor and so we will be relying solely on the content that is available via Khan Academy.
The primary goal is foster a culture whereby 1) The participants can take the agency over their own learning and b) Start developing confidence, that will hopefully lead them towards mastery in this subject.
As a participant, you can start at any level (starting with Early Math / K-2nd grade) and proceed at a pace that you are comfortable with. To get value out of this pilot project, it would be ideal if participants spend at least 30 to 45 minutes (5 times a week) practicing skill development via Khan Academy.
